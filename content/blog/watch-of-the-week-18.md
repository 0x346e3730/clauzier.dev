## Watch of the Week 18

Welcome to this Watch of the Week !

If you're on socials you must have seen the latest PHP drama : Laravel is evil and harmful. 😱
I've been a Symfony enjoyer since the beginning, but I don't think that any framework is wrong by design. [Brent wrote about it on his blog](https://stitcher.io/blog/things-considered-harmful) and I found the footnote [Laravel at scale](https://usefathom.com/blog/does-laravel-scale) from Fathom fascinating.

Another awesome read I've stumbled upon this week is [Prime Video Tech blog's "Scaling up the Prime Video audio/video monitoring service and reducing costs by 90%](https://www.primevideotech.com/video-streaming/scaling-up-the-prime-video-audio-video-monitoring-service-and-reducing-costs-by-90). It talks about microservices and monoliths 👀

Discord's co-founder Stanislav Vishnevskiy released an article about the way they will be [evolving usernames on Discord](https://discord.com/blog/usernames) and it treats about technical and product debt and how they will handle it in the very near future.

And to wrap it up, Symfony published a blog article about [performance improvements in the upcoming 6.3 release of Symfony](https://symfony.com/blog/new-in-symfony-6-3-performance-improvements) and in combination with Symfony UX 2.8 (see the thread below from Ryan Weaver), I cannot wait to live on the edge and try all those new things !
